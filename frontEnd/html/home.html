About us

We are team Velocirapter and we decided to make and education website that focuses on teaching the game of American football. We implemented a Pixi/JavaScript game which allows you to run different routes and offensive formations against varying defensive formations. We are rag-tag team that joined forces at the last minute. we hope that you learn something from our website!
